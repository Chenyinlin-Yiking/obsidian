## 换元积分

### 万能公式

#### 方法
$$
\begin{align}
令u = tan\frac{x}{2} \\
sinx = \frac{2u}{1+u^2}\\
cosx = \frac{1-u^2}{1+u^2}\\
tanx = \frac{2u}{1+u^2}

\end{align}
$$
