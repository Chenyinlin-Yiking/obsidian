## 经典句型 
### 1. sth pose a huge threat to sb/sth  某事对某人 (某物)造成巨大威胁

实战操练：
Water pollution, an increasingly serious problem, poses huge threat to our environment
水污染，作为一个越来越严重的问题，对我们的环境造成了威胁。

替换词：
1. pose —— bring
2. huge —— enormous，gigantic，tremendous
3. Increasingly (越来越)——more and more, growing 

注意：
increasingly 后面接形容词或副词
growing 后面接名词（a growing number of）

### 2. The news that + 句子 has spread + 地点 —— 某新闻已经在某地点传播开来。

名词+句子——句子作为同位语修饰名词

实战操练：
The news that problematic vaccines are widely used in epidemic-prevention field has spread swiftly across the society
问题疫苗在传染病预防领域广泛使用的消息已经在整个社会迅速传播开来。

替换词：
swiftly —— quickly 的高级表达
popular —— 受欢迎的，流行的
prevalent、prevailing——普遍的，常见的 

### 3. Those who do sth will be severely punished 做某事的人会受到严厉惩罚

出现定语从句、后置定语，一般会把 people 改为 those
(People) Those who are study abroad come to return to China.

**被动语态是写作常用语态，能用就多用。**

实战操练：
Those who produce and sell fake and inferior products will be severely punished.（与 honesty ，integrity 诚信话题有关）
制造和销售假冒伪劣商品的人将会被严厉惩罚。

fake and inferior —— 假冒伪劣

### 4. The most striking feature of this drawing is that  这幅图画最明显的特征是

替换词：
Striking—— obvious, noticeable, glaring, remarkable, pronounced

实战操练
The most striking feature of this drawing is that a group of graduates are confused about how to opt for their future paths correctly and reasonably.
这幅图画最明显的特征是一群毕业生对于如何正确而合理地选择他们的未来感到困惑。


### 5. What sparks our attention is that...  引起我们关注的是

替换词：
sparks —— stimulate, inspire, provoke

动词后面接一个句子，是主语从句哦。

实战操练
What sparks our attention is that pirated books are gaining increasing popularity in our socity. 引起我们关注的是盗版图书在我们社会中越来越普遍。

其中 gain increasing popularity —— 越来越普遍。

### 6. Apparently, the author of this picture aims at reminding sb of the significance of sth/doing sth.  显然，这位图画绘制者的目的是提醒某人 (某事)/做某事的重要性
图画写作中一般用 creator 而不用 author

替换词：
Aim at —— be aimed at doing, be disigned to do, be aimed to do

实战操练
Apparently, the author of this picture aims at reminding college students of the significance of physical exercises. 显然，这位图画绘制者的目的是提醒大学生身体锻炼的重要性

### 7. It is beyond doubt that ... 毫无疑问的是（毋庸置疑的是）
同义句：
There is no denying (the fact) that...
Undoubtedly, ...

实战操练：
It is beyongd doubt that Internet addiction has a negative effect  on students' study and life. 毫无疑问的是，网瘾对于学生们的学习和生活

替换词：
产生 has —— exert, bring about
Negative —— adverse
N.(名词) and n.(名词) —— and 可替换为 as well as

### 8.  The emergence of ... can be attributed to a couple of relevan reasons (as follows) 某现象的出现可以被归因于几个相关原因（如下）

首先：Firstly, First of all, To begin with, First and foremost, In the first place, Initially, On the top of the list is (that)

其次：Secondly, In addition, Additionally, What's more, More importantly, Plus

最终：Thirdly, Lastly, Last but not least, In the end, Eventually, At last

一方面 ... 另一方面 ... on the one hand, ... , on the other hand, ... ;  for one thing, ... , for another, ... :

实战操练：
The emergence of the problem of food safety can be attributed to a couple of relevant reasons.
食品安全问题的出现能够被归因于几个相关原因。

### 9.  ... serve (或 act) as a catalyst for (sth / doing sth) ... —— 对 ... 起到促进的作用

catalyst —— 催化剂

同意词组：be beneficial to == be good to

实战操练：
Optimism serves as a catalyst for overcoming difficulties and going out from adversities.
乐观精神对克服困难和走出逆境起到促进作用。

### 10. Countless  examples indicate that ... 无数的证据表明（宾从）
同义句：It can be indicated by countless examples that ...（主从）

实战操练：
Countless examples indicate that irrational or improper development plans hinde a young person's career progress. 无数的例子表明不合理以及不理性的发展计划阻碍了一个年轻人的职业进步。

Young person's 有个点需要注意：如果句中是阻碍了很多年轻人，那么应该写为 young persons‘，对于这种类似 person 的单数名词，要先把它变成复数 persons 然后再加 's ，只不过这里把后面的's 省略了。

### 11. It is essential for sb to do sth 对某人来说做某事是必要的

**重要的**
Important, significant, vital, crucial, critical, fundamental, principal, essential, of great (utmost) importance, of great (utmost) significance

实战操练：
It is essential for contemporary college students to be exposed to the society. 对于当代大学生来说，接触社会是必要的。

Be exposed to 意为暴露于，但是后面既可以跟褒义词也可以是贬义词，具体翻译需要更具语境 

### 12. (People should 可省略) Keep in mind that only by doing sth can (we / our nation) embrace a promising future. 人们应该牢记：只有做某事，我们 (我们国家)才会拥有一个美好的未来

实战操练：
People should keep in mind that only by preserving traditional culture can our nation embrace a promising future. 人们应该牢记：只有保护传统文化，我们国家才会拥有一个美好的未来。


### 13. sb in mounting numbers are aware of the necessity of doing sth. 越来越多的某人意识到做某事的必要性

实战操练：
Parents in mounting numbers are aware of the necessity of cultivating/(developing) their kid's independence.
越来越多父母意识到培养他们孩子的独立性的必要性。

### 14. When it comes to the proportion of sb who do sth, we can find the percentage is ... 当谈论到做某事的人的比例时，我们能够发现这个比例是 ...

实战操练：
When it comes to the proportion of freshmen who take part in part-time jobs, we can find the percentage is 45%.
当谈论到大一学生参加兼职工作的比例时，我们发现数据为 45%

大一：freshman
大二：sophomore
大三：junior
大四：senior
（没有大五的说法）

替换：
1. When it comes to == In terms of
2. 如果 freshman 换为 people ，可以把 people 换为 those，who 省略后面的动词根据主被动添加 ing 或者 ed 变为主语
3.  可以在最后 is 后面加上修饰词 rising, declining

### 15. In this day and age, it would not be surprising to find that ... becomes a prevailing trend among sb. 当今，发现某现象在某人中变成一个流行的趋势并不惊讶

替换：
1. In this day and age: now, nowadays, at present

实战操练：
In this day and age, it would not be surprising to find that worshiping idols becomes a prevailing trend among young adults. 
当今，在年轻人中，崇拜明星的现象变成一个流行的趋势并不惊讶。

### 16.  ... make it necessary for sb to do sth. 某种现状使某人做某事成为必要——（可以同句型 11 一起复习）

It 在这里做一个**形式宾语**

实战操练：
Increasing fierce competition in current society and growing pressure make it necessary for college students to enhance their competitiveness through doing part-time jobs.
当今社会，日益激烈的竞争和不断增大的压力使得大学生有必要通过参加兼职工作来提升他们的竞争力。

替换：
1. Enhance —— promote, boost (用的较多), elevate
2. Competitiveness —— competitive edges (竞争优势), skills, ability
3. Through —— via (等同于 through 和 by ，用它不需要区分前两者用法)

### 17. ... I do deem, is a dominating factor that must be pointed out. 我的确认为，... 是一个必须指出的主要原因

实战操练：
Lack of rigid supervision, i do deem, is a dominating factor that must be pointed out.
我的的确认为，缺乏严格的监管是一个必须指出的主要原因。

### 18. Therefore, it is time for Chinese people, adults or old folks, to do sth. 因此，中国人无论是年轻人还是老年人，是该做某事的时候了

It is time | for sb to do sth/ for sth/ that sb did sth 虚拟语气，代表这件事之前没做)

实战操练：
Therefore, it is time for Chinese people, adults or old folks, to behave properly. 
因此，中国人无论是年轻人还是老年人，是该规范自己行为的时候了。

替换：
Therefore —— Accordingly, so, Thus, Hence, As a result  

So 一般不放在句首，用于连接两个句子，**且前一句的末尾是逗号不是句号**，例如：
He made great efforts in the examination, so he was admitted to Peking University.
但如果前一句末尾是句号，那么用其他几个连词：Therefore + 逗号， Accordingly + 逗号, Thus, Hence, As a result + 逗号，注意 Thus 和 Hence 后面一般不接逗号，而且注意大写。
如果前一句末尾是分号，那么同上用法，只不过开头字母小写。

### 19. ... is beneficial not only to ... but also to ... 某事不仅对... 有好处，还对... 有好处

实战操练：
University ranking is beneficial not only to students' acquisition of knowledge, but also to a college's long-term development.
大学排名不仅对学生获取知识有帮助，还对一所大学的长期发展是有好处的。

替换：
Not only —— not just, not simply, not merely
But also —— but, also, but ... as well

### 20. Accordingly, the significance of ... deserves to be repeatedly emphasized in our society.  因此，... 的重要性应该在我们社会中反复强调

实战操练：
Accordingly, the significance of life-long learning, deserves to be repeatedly emphasized in our society. 
因此，终身学习的重要性应该在我们社会中反复强调。

### 21. （感谢信多用）Without sth， sb could not have done sth. 如果没有某物，某人就不可能做某事

Without your timely help, I could not have made such a big success.

如果没有创新精神，史蒂夫乔布斯就不可能设计出一系列令人叹为观止的电子产品，并且取得相当巨大的成功。

score —— 取得

### 23. Fortunately, there is a dawning realization among sb that ... 幸运的是，某人开始意识到...

### 24. In the age of ... , sth is booming at an unprecedented rate. 在... 时代，某现象在飞速发展

实战操练：
In the age of mobile internet, virtual shopping is booming at an unprecedented rate.
在移动互联网时代，网络购物以前所未有的速度蓬勃发展

注意：
booming —— 繁荣，发展
Unprecedented —— 史无前例的
Virtue —— 美德
Virtual —— 虚拟的
Virtually —— 实际上
网络学习 —— education online, distant learning, e-learning

**思考：网络购物的好处**
Advantages —— convenient, time-saving, less money for products of high quality
Disadvantages —— fake and inferior products, the leakage of personal information

### 25.  My argument is that publicizing and popularizing sth is not an easy task and demands a range of sensible and concrete moves. 我认为宣传和推广某事并不是一件容易的事情，并且还需要一系列合理而具体的行动。（措施段开头，一般是第三段）

curb —— 控制；抑制

### 44. The same is true of / for ... 同样如此

### 46. Compared with A, B... 与 A 相比，B ...

原句：When B is compared with A, B... ，状语从句中，前后两句如果主语保持一致，可以省去前句主语，前句的动词发生变化，主动语态变现在分词，被动语态只保留过去分词，改完后由于前句已经不是一个完整的句子，可以把 when 省略。

实战操练：
Compared with rural areas, urban areas can provide us with more advanced educational and medical resources.
与农村地区相比，城市地区能够给我们提供更多现金的医疗和教育资源。

替换词：
provide ...with ... == offer sb sth == afford sb sth

例句：
与美国学生相比，中国学生的经济来源很少来自于兼职和奖学金。
Compared with American students, the economical origin of Chinese students ...
**一个很大的问题，前句主语是美国学生，是人，所以后一句的主语也应该是人，即中国学生，如果后句主语想要是中国学生的经济来源，那么前句的主语就要是美国学生的经济来源。**
Compared with American students, Chinese students get few financial supports from part-time jobs and scholarships.

注意：
Few / a few 修饰可数名词
little / a little 修饰不可数名词
